History

Founded in 2005, Koom Valley Rapids has a rich history of providing exceptional white water rafting experiences. From humble beginnings with just a single raft and a passionate team, we have grown into a renowned adventure company with a reputation for delivering thrilling and safe rafting adventures.

Over the years, we have explored and conquered some of the most challenging rivers across the disc, from raging rapids in the heart of Koom Valley to picturesque waterways nestled in the majestic mountains of Lancre. Our team of experienced guides and instructors bring their expertise and love for the sport to ensure each trip is filled with excitement and adventure.

Throughout our history, we have remained committed to the principles of safety, customer satisfaction, and environmental stewardship. We prioritize the well-being of our clients, providing thorough safety briefings, top-quality equipment, and trained staff who are adept at handling any situation that may arise on the water.

At Koom Valley Rapids, we believe that white water rafting is not just a recreational activity but a transformative experience that fosters teamwork, personal growth, and a deep connection with nature. We are proud to have shared these remarkable moments with countless adventurers who have joined us on this incredible journey.

As we continue to evolve and expand our offerings, our commitment to delivering exceptional rafting experiences remains unwavering. Join us today and become a part of our history as we embark on new adventures and create memories that will last a lifetime.
